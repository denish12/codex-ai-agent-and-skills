# Platform Strategy

## When to Use
- At the **start of content strategy** — after `$audience-analysis`, before `$content-calendar`.
- When **scaling** — adding new platforms to existing ones.
- When **revising strategy** — metrics are not met, rebalancing is needed.
- With **limited resources** — choosing where to focus and what to abandon.

## Input

| Field | Required | Description |
|------|:-----------:|----------|
| Target Audience Personas | ✅ | From `$audience-analysis` — who, where they spend time, platform preferences |
| Business Goals | ✅ | Awareness / leads / sales / community — what is the priority |
| Budget | ✅ | $0 (organic only) / specific amount (paid + organic). Impacts the strategy |
| Content Resources | ✅ | How many content units per week can realistically be produced (1 person vs team) |
| Current Channels | ⬚ | Where there is already presence, audience, metrics |
| `$competitor-content-analysis` Result | ⬚ | Where competitors are, where the gaps are |
| Geo | ⬚ | Target markets (impacts choice: WeChat vs Instagram, VK vs Telegram) |

> If budget and resources are not specified — **request them**. A strategy of "5 platforms × 5 posts/week" without resources is unrealistic.

| Platform | Strength | Funnel | Audience (General) | Organic Potential (2025-2026) |
|-----------|----------------|---------|-------------------|-----------------------------------|
| **Instagram** | Visual content, brand awareness, community | TOFU / MOFU | 18-44, B2C, lifestyle | Medium (Reels > Feed) |
| **TikTok** | Virality, reach, young audience | TOFU | 16-34, B2C | High |
| **LinkedIn** | B2B, expertise, networking | MOFU / BOFU | 25-55, B2B, professionals | High (Text > Video) |
| **Telegram** | Loyal audience, content depth | MOFU / BOFU | 18-45, tech-savvy, CIS | High |
| **YouTube** | Long-form content, SEO, evergreen | TOFU / MOFU | 18-54, all | Medium (requires production) |
| **Twitter/X** | News, opinions, real-time | TOFU | 20-45, tech, media | Low (algorithm unpredictable) |
| **Email** | Conversion, retention, personalization | MOFU / BOFU | Subscribers (any age) | High (owned base) |
| **Blog / SEO** | Search traffic, evergreen | TOFU / MOFU | Depends on niche | High (long-term) |

## Protocol

### Step 1 — Audience → Platforms
1. For each persona from `$audience-analysis` — determine where they spend time (platform preferences).
2. Match with the platforms table.
3. List **all potential platforms** (long list).

### Step 2 — Filtering by Goals and Funnel
1. For each business goal — determine the funnel stage:
   - Awareness → TOFU platforms (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, Blog).
   - Leads → MOFU/BOFU (LinkedIn, Telegram, Email).
   - Sales → BOFU (Email, Telegram, retargeting).
   - Community → MOFU (Telegram, Discord, groups).
2. Filter out platforms that do not match the goals.

### Step 3 — Resource Estimation
1. Determine **content capacity**: how many content units can realistically be produced per week.
2. Estimate the requirements of each platform:

| Platform | Minimum Frequency | Effort per Unit | Required Content/Week |
|-----------|--------------------|--------------------|------------------------|
| Instagram | 3-5/week | Medium (text + visual) | 3-5 |
| TikTok | 3-7/week | Medium (video) | 3-7 |
| LinkedIn | 2-3/week | Medium (text) | 2-3 |
| Telegram | 3-5/week | Low (text) | 3-5 |
| YouTube | 1-2/week | High (video production) | 1-2 |
| Email | 1-2/week | Medium | 1-2 |
| Blog | 1-2/month | High (longread + SEO) | 0.25-0.5 |

3. **Rule:** Total content/week across all platforms ≤ content capacity. If not enough — reduce platforms.

### Step 4 — Prioritization
Rank platforms by 4 criteria (1-5 points):

| Criterion | Description |
|----------|----------|
| **Audience** | Is our target audience present there? (from `$audience-analysis`) |
| **Goals** | Does the platform solve our business problem? |
| **Competition** | Is there a gap left by competitors? (from `$competitor-content-analysis`) |
| **Resource constraint** | Can we produce content at the required frequency? |

Result:
- **Priority 1 (Core):** 1-2 platforms — main focus, maximum resources.
- **Priority 2 (Growth):** 1-2 platforms — development, moderate resources.
- **Priority 3 (Experimental):** 0-1 platform — test, minimum resources.
- **Postponed:** the rest — not now, return to them when resources grow.

### Step 5 — Strategy per Platform
For each selected platform:
1. **Role in funnel** — TOFU / MOFU / BOFU.
2. **Formats** — which content types (reels, posts, carousels, video, articles).
3. **Frequency** — publications/week.
4. **Best time** — based on platform data or `$audience-analysis`.
5. **Tone** — adapting ToV for the platform (LinkedIn is more formal than Instagram).
6. **Paid / Organic** — promotion strategy.

### Step 6 — Paid vs Organic
For each priority platform:

| Type | When | Budget | Goal |
|-----|-------|--------|------|
| **Organic** | Always — foundational content | $0 | Awareness, engagement, community |
| **Paid boost** | Best organic posts | 10-20% of budget | Scaling what already works |
| **Paid ads** | Specific campaigns | 80-90% of budget | Leads, conversions, retargeting |

> If budget = $0: organic only. Focus on 1-2 platforms with maximum organic potential.

### Step 7 — Cross-posting
1. Determine **content core**: the main platform where the original is created.
2. Determine **adaptations**: what and how to change for other platforms.

| Original → Adaptation | What Changes |
|-----------------------|-------------|
| Instagram Reel → TikTok | Remove watermark, adapt caption |
| Instagram Carousel → LinkedIn | Make text post + PDF carousel |
| Blog → Telegram | Summary of key points |
| YouTube → Instagram Reels | Cut best moments (≤ 60 sec) |
| Email → Telegram | Adapt format, remove HTML |

3. **Rule:** Cross-posting ≠ copy-pasting. Each platform requires adaptation.

### Step 8 — Growth Roadmap
Phased plan by quarters:

| Phase | Period | Platforms | Focus |
|------|--------|-----------|-------|
| **Launch** | Q1 | 1-2 Core platforms | Gather initial audience, find working formats |
| **Grow** | Q2 | Core + 1 Growth | Scale what works. Test a new platform |
| **Scale** | Q3-Q4 | Core + Growth + Experimental | Optimize, add paid, automate cross-posting |

> Roadmap is adapted based on metrics from each quarter.

## Metrics and Benchmarks

| Platform | Key Metric | Benchmark (Average) | Good | What it Measures |
|-----------|-----------------|--------------------|---------|--------------| 
| **Instagram** | ER (Engagement Rate) | 1-3% | > 3% | Engagement |
| **Instagram** | Reach Rate | 10-20% of followers | > 30% | Reach |
| **TikTok** | Views / Followers | 10-30% | > 50% | Virality |
| **LinkedIn** | Impressions / Followers | 5-15% | > 20% | Reach |
| **Telegram** | Views / Subscribers | 30-50% | > 60% | Readthrough |
| **YouTube** | CTR thumbnail | 2-5% | > 5% | Clickability |
| **YouTube** | Avg view duration | 40-50% | > 50% | Retention |
| **Email** | Open Rate | 20-25% | > 30% | Deliverability + subject |
| **Email** | CTR | 2-3% | > 5% | Conversion |
| **Blog** | Organic traffic / month | Depends on niche | 10-20% MoM growth | SEO growth |

## Validation (Quality Gate)

The strategy is considered ready if:

- [ ] Personas are matched with platforms (where the audience spends time)
- [ ] Platforms are prioritized (Core / Growth / Experimental / Postponed)
- [ ] Total frequency ≤ content capacity (realism)
- [ ] For each platform: role in funnel, formats, frequency, time
- [ ] Paid/Organic strategy is defined (accounting for budget)
- [ ] Cross-posting is planned (what to adapt, no copy-pasting)
- [ ] Metrics and target values are locked in
- [ ] Growth roadmap defined (phases, quarterly plan)
- [ ] No platforms without justification (each has a reason why it's there)
- [ ] Approved by user

## Anti-patterns

| Mistake | Why it's Bad | How to do it Right |
|--------|-------------|---------------|
| 5+ platforms without resources | Spread too thin, 0 results | Better to do 2 platforms well than 5 poorly |
| All platforms = TOFU | No conversion, only reach | At least 1 MOFU/BOFU platform (email, Telegram) |
| Cross-posting = copy-paste | Watermarks, unadapted format, penalty | Each platform = adaptation |
| No budget context | Strategy with paid for a $0 budget | Explicitly state paid/organic for each platform |
| Frequency without resources | "7 posts/week" → burnout in 2 weeks | Frequency ≤ real content capacity |
| Choosing platform "because it's trendy" | Audience isn't on TikTok, but everyone says to post | Data-driven choice: Audience + Goals + Resources |
| Same tone everywhere | LinkedIn tone on TikTok = unnatural | Adapt ToV to the platform |
| No roadmap | Attempting to launch everything at once | Phased growth: Launch → Grow → Scale |
